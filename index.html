Hey Harshini… there’s something for you ❤️

A letter… just for you

From Bhuvanakruthi

Hey Harshini…
There’s something special just for you ❤️

Just a small journey…
filled with a few things I wanted you to know.

We’ve known each other for so long…
from school days to now…
and somehow, we’ve always been there in each other’s lives ❤️

We were never just friends…
you were my best friend… my sister…
someone I could share everything with.

We’ve had so many memories together —
laughing, teasing, making fun of each other…

And yeah… we fought a lot too 😄
but not even a single fight ever lasted long.
Somehow, one of us would always come back…
and we’d be okay again.

We’ve had so many moments together…
the kind that only we would understand 😄

Sitting together every single day…
doing everything side by side…
you were literally my crime partner.

And those last bench days…
they were something else.
Sitting there together…
doing random things, realizing random stuff…
and just laughing endlessly.

We always found a way
to make even the simplest moments fun.

And honestly…
nothing ever felt boring when you were there.

You’re not just my best friend…
you’re so much more than that.

You’re my comfort…
my safe place…
someone I can always be myself with.

I respect your dreams…
everything you want to become…
and I’ll always support you in it.

No matter what happens…
I’ll always be there for you —
when you’re happy…
when you’re low…
when you just need someone to listen…
or even just a shoulder to lean on.

As your best friend,
your biggest supporter,
and your forever cheerleader ❤️

And no matter where life takes us…
I know we’ll always find our way back to each other.

And then… things changed.

For the first time…
we weren’t together anymore.
Different colleges… different lives…
and suddenly, everything felt so different.

We used to talk less…
sometimes not even for days…
and honestly, that hurt more than I expected.

I never thought there would be days
where we wouldn’t talk at all.

I missed you in the smallest things…
in my classes… in random moments…
even while talking to others.

There was this one time…
during a concert in college…
when they played a friendship song…
and I actually cried… thinking about you.

We had misunderstandings…
we fought…
but somehow, we still found our way back.

Because no matter the distance…
what we have never really changed.

And I still miss you…
more than I can explain 💔

And finally…
it’s your day ❤️

Happy Birthday, Harshini 🎉

I don’t even know how to put this into words…
but I’m so grateful to have you in my life.

I just want you to always believe in yourself…
to be confident, strong, and brave
in everything you do.

Take care of yourself…
stay healthy, stay happy…
and keep growing into the amazing person you are.

And yes… maybe control that little bit of temper sometimes 😄

You deserve a life
that is beautiful, peaceful, and full of happiness…

And no matter where we are…
just don’t forget —
I’m always there for you.

Now go… enjoy your day 😄
and don’t forget…
you’re still my favourite idiot ❤️
